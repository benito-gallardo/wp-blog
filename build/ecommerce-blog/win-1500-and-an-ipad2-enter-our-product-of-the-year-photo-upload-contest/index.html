Want to send some publicity to your site while earning a shot to win some awesome prizes? Upload a picture of your online store’s favorite product for your big break!


What would you do with a $1500 Visa gift card and an iPad2? Enter our Product of the Year Photo Upload Contest for your chance to find out!

All you have to do is upload an image of your online store’s best product, whether it’s your top seller, your quirkiest item, or any product listed for sale.   • Second Prize: $1000 Visa Gift Card + iPad2 ($499 value)

Don’t delay! The sooner you enter, the more time you have to collect votes! GOOD LUCK!
