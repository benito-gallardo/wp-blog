Shipwire, eBridge, Stamps.com and Verisign join Volusion to streamline online merchants’ business operations.

.

As the leading provider of ecommerce software and services for online merchants, we’re very proud to announce the addition of four new partners to our network at Internet Retailer’s 2010 Conference and Exposition. Shipwire, eBridge, Stamps.com and Verisign have all partnered with us to increase their revenue opportunities while helping streamline business operations for our customers.

Our partner program already includes more than fifteen top companies from their respective industries, including Microsoft, FedEx, UPS and QuickBooks. The addition of the following companies to ourpartner network will provide best-of-breed distribution, back-office and infrastructure resources to all of our 20,000+ ecommerce clients.

“At Volusion, we’ve always known that great companies need truly great partnerships. Collaboration and alliances allow multiple parties to focus on what they do best while collectively enhancing the overall ecommerce experience for our clients,” said Clay Olivier, Volusion COO and CMO. “We’re committed to continually finding synergies with leading companies to provide valuable resources for our customers. We anticipate adding new efficiencies that will greatly enhance the Volusion experience as a result of working with these new partners.”

.

Here’s a little more information about our new partners:


Shipwire provides an instant integration to automate Volusion order fulfillment and shipping. Shipwire provides ecommerce order fulfillment services powered by a global warehouse network and its Store-Sell-Ship™ platform. With warehouses across the U.S., Canada and the U.K., Shipwire allows merchants to drastically reduce shipping costs by locating inventory closer to buyers.

.


With eBridge Volusion solutions, customer and order information is automatically entered into the back-office financial system from a Volusion merchant’s database. In turn, product information and order status updates are automatically entered into the Volusion interface to provide customers with essential up-to-date information.

.


Stamps.com’s online postage service enables Volusion customers to print U.S. Postal Service -approved postage with just a PC, printer and Internet connection, right from their home or office. The company targets its services to small businesses and home offices, and currently has PC Postage partnerships with Avery Dennison, Microsoft, HP, Office Depot, the U.S. Postal Service and others.

.


VeriSign provides trusted infrastructure services to websites, enterprises, ecommerce service providers and individuals. The company’s digital certificate provides the critical web identity, authentication and transaction infrastructure that online businesses require to conduct secure ecommerce and communications.

.

We’re excited to have these great companies on board and share this sentiment with our partners. Here’s what some of their experienced leaders had to say about our new relationship:

Steve Bielawski of eBridge Software Business Development:
“By joining forces with Volusion, we are able to deliver over 37 accounting integrations to online business owners. This enables merchants to seamlessly and automatically transfer data between their accounting systems and the Volusion platform. We’re excited to offer the financial technology that online merchants need to eliminate the time-consuming manual data input process.”

Nate Gilmore, Shipwire VP of Business Development and Marketing:
“We are excited to partner with Volusion, an ecommerce leader. Our alliance enables Volusion merchants to automate order fulfillment and grow their businesses. The free Shipwire connection provides automated order fulfillment, real-time inventory levels from Shipwire warehouses to merchants’ Volusion accounts and seamless order tracking. We’re pleased to provide an integration which allows Volusion merchants to focus on their business and remove the hassles of storage and shipping.”

.

We look forward to working with each of these groups to extend and enhance our platform, all in the name of helping you succeed online.

.
