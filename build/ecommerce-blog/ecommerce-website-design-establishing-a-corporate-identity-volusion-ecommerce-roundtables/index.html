Coca-Cola. Apple. McDonald’s.


When you think of these mega-brands, you can quickly envision their logo, color scheme and style. You can probably even conjure memories and feelings about these brands based on your interactions with them.

Guess what? Your online business can do the same, so long as you design your ecommerce website with a sound strategy.

Join us for this week’s Ecommerce Roundtable as two of our professional web designers, Jessica and Sarah, walk me through the basics of designing your ecommerce site to build your brand. If you have any questions, simply leave a comment below.
