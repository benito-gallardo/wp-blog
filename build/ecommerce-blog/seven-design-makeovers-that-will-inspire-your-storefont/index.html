Curious about custom ecommerce design? Take a look at some of the latest eye-catching creations our talented team of designers has made for our Volusion merchants.

For those of you whose summer plans include freshening up your storefront, we have put together an inspirational showcase of seasonal stores. At Volusion, we have a team dedicated to creating custom design solutions for your business; here is some of our favorite custom work:

 

Start the summer off right with natural, vegan beauty and bath care products from Lulu Blossom. Their all-natural skincare line is made with the freshest and finest ingredients.

Lulu Blossom’s pin-up logo was full of enough personality to fill the entire page. Every creative decision their designer made helped to bring that personality to all corners of their online store, from the tropical flower colors to the playful patterns and icons.

 

If you’re looking for a new bag to carry with you on your next vacation, Stag Designs is just the shop for you. Their in-house designers work with master artisans to produce original, rustic collections.

The site’s designer brought the rustic detailing of Stag Design’s products into the page itself, with patterning and leather-inspired elements that paid homage to their collection without distracting from their wonderful and detail-focused product images.

 

If you’re planning on horseback riding this summer, Equezon should be your first stop. They specialize in high-end riding and horse care products, from tack and equipment to horse blankets and riding apparel.

Volusion’s design team created stitched leather elements and a textured background, which add a touch of the product line to their site’s navy blue brand coloring.

 

If you’re planning on a staycation, it might be time to upgrade your bedding. Check out Purple Rose Home for the most luxurious, shabby chic home furnishings. Don’t forget to visit their store, located in an 18th century colonial-style home in Northborough, MA.

Purple Rose Home’s designer brought the shabby chic aesthetic of their storefront to their website. Subtle flower prints, creamy coloring and the texture of peeling paint work together with the site’s imaginative text to inspire their customers.

 

Summer means road trips! Accessorize and maintain your vehicle with offerings from 707 Motoring. They hand-pick the highest quality aftermarket auto body parts at the lowest possible price, so you know you’re getting a deal.

This site’s designer incorporated light blue and silver elements to add a punchy color contrast that expanded on 707’s logo design. The design catches the eye while still allowing the more dramatically colorful products to take center stage.

 

Fixing up the old deck out back? You’re going to need to make sure your tools are in tip top shape, and HL Supply has you covered. They specialize in high-quality small engine parts for forestry, construction, lawn and garden equipment.

In addition to adding attractive blue accents, our designers created a faded wood grain background for HL Supply. This background calls to mind the types of projects that their customers will be working on, so that their interests share the stage with HL Supply’s products.

 

Mystique will make sure that you are beach ready with the most stylish sandals. Each pair of sandals is designed by Yamin – one half of the husband and wife duo who runs Mystique – and are crafted with natural leather soles, Czech crystal chains and the highest quality glass stones, turquoise and shells.

Sometimes less is more. Mystique’s designer anticipated seasonal product changes and fluctuating color trends and worked to create a theme with an ambient allure. The appealing fonts and unobtrusive colors keep the site interesting without overpowering the products or clashing with every-changing featured images.

 

Interested in working with one of our designers to improve your store? You should check out our custom design packages and a la carte design services!
